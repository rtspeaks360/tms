TRANSPORT MANAGEMENT

Let's see!

The Transportation Management System or TMS is basically a Web Application that has been designed to help the Transport Department of any Institution or a Conglomerate. It helps the Transport officer of the body to manage various records. To name a few to keep a track of students, vehicles, routes, drivers and the fuel records of the department. About the tech, TMS is designed on top of CSS's Twitter Bootstrap framework and the backend of the application is runing on a MICRO-FRAMEWORK for Python named Flask.
